ABOUT SDG 11

SDG 11 aims to make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable.

With rapid urbanization, it's crucial to ensure that cities grow in ways that protect the environment.

These improvements help support communities and improve overall quality of life.

55%

of the world's population live in cities

1B+

people live in slums or informal settlements

70%

of global carbon emissions come from cities

TARGETS

Targets highlighting the priorities for sustainable urban development and improved quality of life in cities.

SAFE AND AFFORDABLE HOUSING

By 2030, ensure access for all to adequate, safe and affordable housing and basic services and upgrade slums.

AFFORDABLE AND SUSTAINABLE TRANSPORT SYSTEMS

By 2030, provide access to safe, affordable, accessible and sustainable transport systems for all, improving road safety and public transport, especially for vulnerable groups.

INCLUSIVE AND SUSTAINABLE URBANIZATION

By 2030, enhance participatory and sustainable human settlement planning in all countries.

PROTECT THE WORLD’S CULTURAL AND NATURAL HERITAGE

Strengthen efforts to safeguard global cultural and natural heritage.

REDUCE THE ADVERSE EFFECTS OF NATURAL DISASTERS

By 2030, significantly reduce deaths, impacts, and economic losses caused by disasters, especially for vulnerable communities.

REDUCE THE ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT OF CITIES

By 2030, reduce the environmental impact of cities with focus on air quality and waste management.

PROVIDE ACCESS TO SAFE AND INCLUSIVE GREEN AND PUBLIC SPACES

By 2030, provide universal access to safe, inclusive and accessible public green spaces.

STRONG NATIONAL AND REGIONAL DEVELOPMENT PLANNING

Support positive links among urban, peri-urban and rural areas through national and regional planning.

IMPLEMENT POLICIES FOR INCLUSION, RESOURCE EFFICIENCY AND DISASTER RISK REDUCTION

Increase cities adopting climate adaptation, disaster resilience, and sustainable urban planning policies.

SUPPORT LEAST DEVELOPED COUNTRIES IN SUSTAINABLE AND RESILIENT BUILDING

Assist least developed countries in building sustainable, resilient structures using local materials.

CHALLENGES WE FACE

Key Issues Affecting Sustainable Cities Today.

Urban Pollution

Increasing vehicles and industries contribute to unsafe air and noise levels.

Overpopulation

Uncontrolled growth leads to congestion, lack of housing, and unsafe living conditions.

Waste Management

Poor waste disposal harms city health and natural ecosystems.

Innovative Approaches to Build Sustainable Cities.

Green Infrastructure

Expanding green spaces, sustainable transport, and nature-based solutions.

Smart Waste Systems

Smarter recycling, reduced landfill use, and energy-efficient waste solutions.

Affordable Housing

Creating inclusive, safe, sustainable homes for growing urban populations.

Renewable Energy

Using solar, wind, and clean energy sources to power sustainable cities.

Improving early warning systems and building safer, resilient communities.
